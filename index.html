My Scientific Life Theory

Author: Golden (Chin-Yuan Hwaung)

This paper presents a comprehensive scientific life theory that explores the origin, mechanism, and essence of life. It combines concepts from physics, biology, consciousness studies, and philosophy to form a unified, alternative perspective on what life is and how it emerges.

The theory aims to offer a new framework that bridges the gap between physical sciences and the phenomena of life, including consciousness and the soul.
